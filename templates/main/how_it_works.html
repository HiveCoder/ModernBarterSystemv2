How SilkTrade Works

Trade without cash. Build reputation. Grow your network.

1. Create Your Profile

Sign up for free. Add a photo, bio, and location so traders know who they're dealing with. Verified traders get a badge.

2. List Your Items

Upload photos, describe the item's condition and estimated value, and specify what you want in return — or leave it open to offers.

3. Browse & Discover

Search by category, condition, location, value range, and more. Save items to your wishlist to keep track of what interests you.

4. Make an Offer

Found something you want? Select one of your listed items and send a trade offer with a personal message to the seller.

5. Negotiate & Chat

Each trade has a dedicated chat. Discuss details, agree on exchange logistics, and get to know your trading partner.

6. Complete & Review

Once both parties are happy, mark the trade as complete. Earn 10 Trade Credits and leave a review to build your reputation.

Trade Credits System

Earn 10 credits for every completed trade. Credits reflect your experience and trustworthiness on the platform. The more you trade, the higher you climb the leaderboard.
